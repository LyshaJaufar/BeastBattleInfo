ABOUT


Beast Battle is a collectible card game that incorporates strategic deck building elements by implementing trading cards from a best-selling fantasy novel series that is Beast Quest. Initiated in 2018 by founders Lysha and her inferior assistant, Aayan, Beast Battle has approximately sixty-nine million players (more accurately, two.. who are the two founders) as of August 2020, and over 150 Beast Battle cards and counting.
